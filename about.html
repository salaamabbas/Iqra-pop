About Iqra Pop

Dedicated to providing quality Islamic education online since 2020

Our Story

Iqra Pop was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to make quality Islamic education accessible to everyone, regardless of their location or background. Our journey began in Uganda, where we identified a need for structured, accessible Quranic education that could reach students beyond traditional classroom settings.

Founder's Message

Sheikh Abdulsalaam Abbas Ssenkindu

Assalamu Alaikum,

Iqra Pop was founded to make quality Islamic education accessible to all. The name "Iqra" (Read!) honors the first word revealed to Prophet Muhammad (PBUH), emphasizing the importance of knowledge in Islam.

Our dedicated teachers guide students in Quran recitation, memorization, and Quranic Arabic with personalized attention. We believe in nurturing both mind and soul through quality Qur'an education that's accessible regardless of location or financial circumstances.

We invite you to join our community and grow with us in knowledge and faith.

Sheikh Abdulsalaam Abbas Ssenkindu
Founder, Iqra Pop

Our Mission

Our goal is to foster a strong bond between servants and their Creator. We aim to achieve this by providing accessible and engaging Quranic education while considering the financial status for all. Through personalized instruction and modern teaching methods, we strive to make Islamic learning a joyful and transformative experience.

Our Vision

We envision becoming a leading online Qur'an school providing quality education that strengthens the relationship between a servant and The Creator. We aim to create a global community of learners who are confident in their Islamic knowledge and practice, and who can pass on this knowledge to future generations.

Our Values

  • Flexibility: Adapting to each student's learning pace and schedule
  • Excellence: Maintaining the highest standards in teaching and curriculum
  • Teamwork: Collaborating to create the best learning environment
  • Integrity: Upholding Islamic principles in all our interactions
  • Progress: Continuously improving our methods and resources
